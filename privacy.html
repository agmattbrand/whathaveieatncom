Information We Collect

Information You Provide to Us

We collect information that you provide directly to us, including:

  • Account Information: When you register for an account, we collect your email address, username, first name, last name, and password.
  • Profile Information: You may choose to provide additional information for your profile, such as a profile picture and biographical information.
  • User Content: We collect the content you create, upload, or share through the App, such as food entries, photos, ratings, reviews, tags, and comments.
  • Communications: We collect information when you contact us directly or communicate with other users through the App.
  • Contacts: If you choose to invite friends to the App, we may request access to your contact list to facilitate the invitation process.

Information We Collect Automatically

When you use the App, we automatically collect certain information, including:

  • Device Information: We collect information about the device you use to access the App, including the hardware model, operating system and version, unique device identifiers, and mobile network information.
  • Location Information: With your permission, we collect precise geolocation information from your device to show you nearby restaurants, personalize your experience, and provide location-based features.
  • Usage Information: We collect information about your use of the App, such as the features you use, the actions you take, the time, frequency, and duration of your activities.
  • Log Information: We collect log information when you use the App, including access times, pages viewed, app crashes, and other system activity.

Information from Third Parties

We may receive information about you from third parties, including:

  • Social Media: If you link your account with a social media platform, we may receive information about you from that platform according to your settings and the privacy policy of that platform.
  • Friends: Other users may provide information about you when they use our App, such as when they tag you in a food entry or invite you to join the App.
  • Service Providers: We may receive information from our service providers who help us with various aspects of our business, such as processing payments or analyzing App usage.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for various purposes, including to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the App and its features
  • Create and manage your account
  • Process and complete transactions
  • Send you technical notices, updates, security alerts, and administrative messages
  • Respond to your comments, questions, and requests
  • Communicate with you about products, services, offers, promotions, and events
  • Personalize your experience and provide content and features that may interest you
  • Facilitate social features of the App, such as sharing food entries and connecting with friends
  • Monitor and analyze trends, usage, and activities in connection with the App
  • Detect, investigate, and prevent fraudulent transactions and other illegal activities
  • Protect the rights, privacy, safety, and property of What Have I Eaten, our users, and others
  • Comply with applicable laws, regulations, and legal processes
